Description:

FreeUse allows users to both list and browse free items being given away in Austin. Thereby reducing waste and landfill burdens. The frontend is built with React/Redux and Material UI. The backend uses Node, Express and MongoDB.

Features:

  • - Full CRUD functionality-allowing users to create, edit and delete their own posts

  • - Paginated display of products

  • - The ability to search items by product title or category tags

  • - Incorporates Google Oauth 2.0 for an easy login option
